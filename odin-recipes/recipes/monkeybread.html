A Delicious Monkey Bread Recipe

What is Monkey Bread?

Monkey Bread is an amazingly delicious dessert that is very easy to make. It is gooey, sweet and nice and hot. You will definitely love this savory treat.

Ingredients

  • 24 Ounces of Biscuit Dough
  • Granulated Sugar and Golden Brown Sugar
  • One Cup of Butter A.K.A Two Sticks
  • Cinnamon

Recipe!!!

  1. First cut all of your dough into small bite sized pieces.
  2. Fill a large plastic bag of both types of sugar and cinnamon. Then put your dough into it and shake it around. Do this with a few pieces at a time.
  3. Grease a fluted tube pan and preheat oven to 350 degrees. Place all of your dough into the pan.
  4. Next you must make the delicious syrup. Place 1/2 cup of the sugar-cinnamon mixture in a small saucepan with brown sugar and butter. Heat gently to boil and then shut off the heat. Mix until it is fully dissolved.
  5. Next drizzle the syrup over the dough in the pan and then bake for 30 minutes.
  6. Let it sit for aboyt five plates and then invert it onto a plate.
  7. Enjoy this yummy treat
