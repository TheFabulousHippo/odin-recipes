Adobo Recipe

Description

Filipino Pork Adobo is a traditional Filipino dish that is absolutely delicious. It consits of very tender pork on a bed of rice.

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Distilled White Vinegar
  • 1 Cup Soy Sauce
  • 1/2 Cup Ketchup
  • 1 Teaspoon Fresh-Ground Black Pepper
  • 1 Tablespoon Minced Garlic
  • 3 Bay Leaves
  • 2 1/2 Pounds Lean Pork, Cut Into 1 Inch Cubes

Steps

  1. Stir together the vinegar, soy sauce, ketchup, garlic, and bay leaves in a large saucepan.
  2. Add the Cubed pork, and bring to a boil over high heat.
  3. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until the pork is tender, about 2 1/2 hours.
  4. Stir occasionally
  5. Enjoy some delicious Adobo!
